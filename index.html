About me

Hi, I'm Alan Warthon, a Camosun College student specializing in Information and Computer System, I love programming, but it wasn't always like that, below I will detail a bit of my previous experience in another of my great passions such as aeronautics. Peruvian by birth, I was born in the city of Lima, capital of Peru. I have worked in the world of aeronautics since 2008, I am an Air Traffic Controller. I have the specialties of aerodrome controller, approach controller and radar air controller. In December 2022 I moved to Canada to study at Camosun College, starting a new stage in my life with the goal of becoming an excellent programmer.
